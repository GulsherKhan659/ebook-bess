The Sparked Remarks


Lil’ Bessie “Bessieki” is a woman defined by her evolution from a "flower child" of the 1960s to a formidable business leader, and finally, to a poet of deep spiritual resonance. Though she began her career as a classically trained artist and educator, the demands of being a single mother led her into the international business world. There, her aristocratic poise and sharp intellect allowed her to navigate global cultures, even as she sacrificed her "esoteric passions" of sculpture and painting to provide for her daughters. Now in her 80s, despite the physical toll of a life of hard work, her creative spirit remains undimmed. Having once burned her early writings out of a sense of inadequacy, she eventually turned to poetry as her "new canvas." These verses—some raw with trauma, others elegant with grace—serve as a roadmap of a soul moving toward "Christness" and greater awareness. Published by her daughters through Twin Dreamers, LLC, this collection is the culmination of a life lived as a "work in progress," proving that even when the body tires, the soul continues its upward climb.
